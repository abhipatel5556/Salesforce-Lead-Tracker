Reporting and Dashboard Notes

Reports:
1. Qualified Leads Report – Displays all leads marked as Qualified with filters for industry and region.
2. Disqualified Leads Report – Tracks leads that failed qualification criteria.

Dashboards:
1. Sales Dashboard – Visual summary of total leads, qualified percentage, conversion rate, and monthly trends.
2. Opportunity Conversion Dashboard – Shows conversion rates and potential revenue from qualified leads.

Purpose:
Enable data-driven decision-making and provide actionable insights to the sales team through real-time analytics.
